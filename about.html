About Hotel R&T

Hotel R&T occupies one of the most prestigious spot in the heart of Bangalore. Located on Maysore Road, the hotel affords a spectacular and panoramic view of the city which is really a big attraction.

The ITPL Bus Station, Whitefiled Railway Station and the major shopping centres are just a small walk away from R&T Hotel, Bangalore. Our vintage hotel prides itself in the ease of connectivity it brings to the table. Each room is elegantly designed and decked with comfortable king-size beds. With all modern amenities at your disposal, your stay with us is guaranteed to be hassle-free.
